Who Am I

Hi!

My name is John David Francis, but everyone calls me JD.

I'm currently a third-year programming student at Southern Methodist University studying Computer Science with a Game Development specialization.

I am many things including: programming student, gaming fanatic, self-proclaimed nerd, e-sports enthusiast, avid coffee drinker, but most importantly:
I'm a passionate gamer that loves making games and websites.

All my life I've been a gamer. Even while in kindergarten you would find me playing games like Diablo 1, Runescape and Super Mario 64. Around the time I was in 3rd grade one of my friends introduced me to the program Gamemaker and even before I knew even what the word programming meant, I started making games. Thus I began on the path to becoming a programmer.

I grew up overseas in France and DRCongo for the majority of my childhood, so I didn't have a lot of opportunities to learn programming in school, so instead I decided to become a self-taught programmer. I picked up Gamemaker really fast, and loved working in it, but I also learned a little Python and C. I've always been good at teach myself things and you'll find me to be a highly self-motivated person. After learning programming in school I also found websites to be another area that I really loved. Both game and web development gave me opportunities to really employ my creative abilities to the maximum.

Not only do I love making games, I also love playing them. So, here are some of my favorite games: DotA 2, Heroes of the Storm, Rocket League, Diablo Series, Call of Duty Series (especially the first Modern Warfare), Hearthstone, Counter Strike: Global Offensive, Starcraft 2, World of Warcraft, Bastion, The Elder Scrolls Series, Dark Souls, and Pokemon Ruby, among many others.
